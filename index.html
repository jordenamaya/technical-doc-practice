Creator

Dragon Ball came to fruition from the great mind of Akira Toriyama. Akira Toriyama created Dragon Ball in 1984, an anime that follows a young man by the name of Goku. Goku goes on adventures and trains to be the greatest warrior ever.

Today, Akira is 68 years old! He has dedicated much of his life to this series, his work has been one of the most influential, iconic, and recognizable content in the history of the world. He is an inspiration to many and he continues to entertain with his story-telling and animation skills.

Characters

There are hundreds if not thousands of characters in the Dragon Ball series, the anime series has been going on for nearly 40 years. The main character of course is Goku, and the Z-Fighters are among the most significant characters to note. This includes Vegeta, Piccolo, Krillin, Gohan, and more. My personal favorite character is Krillin. Krillin is the best friend of Goku, they have trained together from the very beginning. Krillin will likely never have any chance of catching up to Goku's fighting skills and power, but he is by far the strongest human to ever exist among the 13 universes. He does not posess any special powers, but his grit and personality is what separates him from the rest of the population.

Transformations

The most notable transformations in the series are the super saiyan transformations. There are some non-saiyan transformations as well, but they do not carry the same emphasis

Each level up in the super saiyan transformations represents in increase in power, a significant amplifier to things like speed, strength, reflex, and agility.

  1. SSJ- Super Saiyan
  2. SSJ2- Super Saiyan 2
  3. SSG3- Super Saiyan Third Grade
  4. SSJ3- Super Saiyan 3
  5. SSJG- Super Saiyan God
  6. SSGSS- Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan
